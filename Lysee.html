Break from College Apps

11/30/2022

My friend and I had been yearning for a restaurant outing since the summer, but our busy academic schedules and essay writing held us back. It was an uphill task to secure a reservation, and the struggle continued until we finally had a free window. I remember staying up till midnight, precisely when the reservation site opened, determined to snag a coveted spot.

Before heading to the restaurant, we embarked on a delightful journey through nearby bakeries, indulging in scrumptious snacks and savoring every moment. Our destination, Jua, had been on our radar for a while, and it did not disappoint. The experience was nothing short of divine. The sushi and desserts on offer elevated our taste buds, marking a memorable culinary experience that will linger as one of the best in my lifetime.

After our gastronomic adventure, we ventured into the enchanting world of the Flatiron District. It was my first time in New York City at night, and the bustling city lights added an element of thrill and a touch of trepidation. However, the presence of my dear friend provided reassurance, making the exploration of the cityscape all the more exciting.

Our journey took us to the Harry Potter store, just an hour before it closed. We savored the iconic butterbeer, complete with collectible cups. The store's ambiance was nothing short of magical, with wands adorning the space, rekindling cherished memories from our childhoods, where we devoutly devoured the Harry Potter books.

Our culinary exploration continued with visits to Lysee and Dough Donuts, both conveniently located nearby. Lysee's signature cake captivated us with its impeccable design and flavor. It left us eager to return and sample more of their offerings, especially the renowned A La Carte and their famous corn cake as depicted above.

As for Dough Donuts, we were enchanted by the store's inviting ambiance and the generous size of their delectable donuts. We opted to share one, and its satisfying sweetness left us both content.

In sum, our long-awaited restaurant outing turned into a delightful escapade filled with delectable flavors and enchanting city sights. The night was a captivating chapter in our shared adventures, a memory to treasure and a promise for more culinary explorations in the future.
