Knitting

05/30/2020

Presently, I'm proficient in only two knitting patterns, yet the process of creating something functional brings immense satisfaction. The scarf I've recently completed, a testament to my revived interest, will soon become a cherished accessory once the colder weather sets in.

While the pandemic has restricted my ability to socialize and explore the outdoors, it has presented an opportunity for introspection and pursuing neglected hobbies.
