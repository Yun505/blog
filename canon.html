New camera!

8/10/2023

Photography has always held a special place in my heart from a young age. Immersing myself in countless videos featuring professional photographers wielding their high-end cameras fueled my desire to own one immensely. Over the years, I diligently saved my allowance, and the day finally came when I could purchase my very own Canon camera.

It was a hot summer day in Korea when I went to the Canon store, accompanied by my dad. He had a passion for cameras in his youth and offered to assist me in choosing a lens and camera. Although I had my eye on a particular model, I was excited to finally get a camera of my own and eager to listen to his advice to make a wise purchase. Stepping into the Canon store offered a welcome relief from the sweltering sun outside. I spent an hour with the worker, deciding on the camera and the lens, as they showed me some of the cool features that Canon had integrated into the device. My dad was astonished by the various features, such as the camera's ability to automatically connect to the phone for image transfer, removing the hassle of our old camera that required a special plug.

The moment I held it in my hands as I stepped out of the store, I was absolutely elated. Without wasting any time, I delved into the camera's intricate modes and settings, eager to unlock its full potential. The lens quality astounded me, producing stunning shots, particularly in low-light conditions, which was a pleasant surprise.

My newfound passion led me to capture the essence of my friends and family, with my camera becoming an inseparable companion for all my outings. It quickly became an indispensable item whenever I stepped outside my home.

The image I've shared is a snapshot of a picturesque river pathway from a memorable trip with my parents in Korea, a testament to the wonders of my newfound photography skills. While I am still a "noob" at taking pictures, I plan to continue to explore with the flash, aperture, and lighting to capture the moment better.
