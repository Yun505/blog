Balloon

01/13/2024

Had a delightful day catching up with a friend I hadn't seen since Thanksgiving. We scored a reservation at the usually hard-to-get-into Konban for lunch, making the experience even more special. Early in the day, we treated ourselves to some light desserts at Ann Tremet, a spot I'd been eager to visit. The friendly vibe, staff, and delicious desserts at Ann Tremet made it a perfect start to our day, complemented by minimal yet cute decorations.

Next stop was Konban, where the atmosphere transported me back to Korea. The staff was incredibly friendly, and the juicy meat added to the enjoyment. Afterward, we walked to Butterdose for some delectable cream puffs, savoring the different flavors in the charming cafe ambiance. The walk to our next destination, the Balloon Museum, took us through Chinatown, adding to the adventure.

The Balloon Museum was a visual delight with the craziest balloons I've ever seen. Despite the crowd, exploring the installations and capturing cool pictures made it worthwhile. We then visited Katz for a classic sandwich and stumbled upon a quirky rice pudding shop with amusing advertisements. The French toast-flavored rice pudding was a sweet experience I'd been wanting to try for years.

Our journey continued to Anjelly, trending online for their cute bouncy jelly cats. We chose a Christmas-themed vanilla one, spending about 5 minutes in awe of its cuteness and bounce. The cozy vibe at Anjelly added to the enjoyment of our visit. It was a day filled with good food, delightful surprises, and great company.
